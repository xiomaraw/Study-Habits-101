About Me!

Hello! My name is Xiomara Watler and over the course of my high school career I have picked up a few study habits that I believe will help you become a more successful student. By having great study habits they will lead to students succeeding in their field of study. The key to studying isn't about cramming or studying longer for a test, but studying smarter. There are different study habits for many courses and websites that will help you study.

Websites

There are many apparatus' that will help you study and review for your upcoming tests.

  • Crash Course is an educational Youtube channel that has videos on different cources like Astronomy to Computer Science to World history in Arabic!
  • Khan Academy is a educational website that provides free online cources, lessons and practice. As well as, free SAT test practice.
  • Quizlet helps students study and learn content that was created by other users or yourself. You can study your materail by playing games or using flashcards.
  • Spark Notes provides study guides for literature, poetry, film and philosophy. Spark notes expanded to provide subjects for biology, economoics, health, math, sociology etc.

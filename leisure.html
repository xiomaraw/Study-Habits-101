Leisure Time

Whenever I find free time, I make sure I use it wisely because I never know when I'll be free again between school and volleyball. However, I try to follow this schedule when I'm free:

  1. Clean Room
  2. Take a bath with a bath bomb and face mask
  3. Meditate
  4. Listen to peaceful music
  5. Bake brownies or chocolate chip cookies
  6. Watch netflix (currently re-watching Grey's Anatomy) and eat brownies
  7. Light a vanilla scent candle and read
  8. Sleep for as long as I can

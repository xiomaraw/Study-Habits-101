Ways to Study

However there might be an abundance of different subjects, but there are many ways to study

  • Creating diagrams, like mind maps
  • Setting a timer to study each section
  • Skimming through the reading first then going back to connect concepts
  • Reward yourself with treats
  • Turn your phone on "Do not Disturb" to get rid of distracting apps or when you're on your laptop. Extensions, like Forest can also block distracting websites. Everytime you use forest, the forest creators partner up with a tree-planting organization and they will plant a tree somewhere around the world.
  • Having a planner will help you be organized and be able to schedule due dates and tests (Example below).

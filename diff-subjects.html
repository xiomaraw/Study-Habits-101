How I Study for Different Subjects

Math and Science

  • Review previous problems
  • Review notes to make sure I understand key concepts

History

  • When I am assigned to read out of a textbook, I usually outline each section by taking post-it notes and use them to summarize each paragraph
  • I take notes during lectures

English

  • Vocabulary: I use index cards or quizlet
  • Literature: Crash Course or Spark Notes to help me understand the novel in a deeper and more meaningful way
